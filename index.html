Favourite Meal Recipe

YAMARITA

Yamarita originated from the Northern region of Nigeria in West Africa and some parts of Asia. Yamarita is a popular food in the Northern parts of Nigeria. However, they are best enjoyed when served with served fried tomato sauce with all the African flavors in it, tomato ketchup or soya spice.

Ingredients/Measurements

Ingredients Measurements
Peeled yam 4 slices
all-purpose flour/corn flour 2 tbsp
eggs 2
ginger powder ½ tsp
ground pepper ½ tsp
Salt
Vegetable oil

How to prepare

  1. Firstly, peel the yam and cut into desired shape and sizes, thereafter, rinse and drain water.
  2. Pour the yams inside a clean pot and add enough water, salt and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  3. Next, in a bowl pour the flour, ginger power, ground pepper, cube seasoning and mix together, then break the eggs into the flour mixture, add some pinch of salt and whisk all ingredients together for a slightly thick batter.
  4. Deep the yam one after the other into the egg and flour batter mixture, make sure the yam is fully coated.
  5. Lastly, heat up the oil and fry the yam, flip to the other side and fry until golden brown.
  6. Remove from oil into a sieve and allow oil to drain.
